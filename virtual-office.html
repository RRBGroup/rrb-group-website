Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Virtual Office?

A Virtual Office provides businesses with a professional business address, mail handling services, and access to meeting rooms without the need for a physical office space. It's perfect for businesses looking to establish a presence in a prime location while maintaining flexibility.

How does mail handling work?

We receive and manage your business mail at our professional address. You can choose to have your mail forwarded to your preferred address, scanned and emailed to you, or held for collection. Our team ensures secure handling of all your correspondence.

Can I use the meeting rooms?

Yes! Our Virtual Office clients have access to our professional meeting rooms at discounted rates. You can book meeting rooms by the hour or day, with options for small meetings or larger conference spaces.

What are the contract terms?

We offer flexible contract terms starting from as little as one month. There are no long-term commitments required, and you can upgrade or downgrade your package as your business needs change.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there is no setup fee for our Virtual Office services. You only pay for the services you use, making it a cost-effective solution for businesses of all sizes.

Can I receive packages?

Yes, we can receive and handle packages on your behalf. We'll notify you when packages arrive and can arrange for forwarding or collection according to your preferences.
