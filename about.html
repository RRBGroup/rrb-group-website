Our Leadership Team

Brayan Rodriguez

Founder

Brayan Rodriguez is the founder of RRB Group, bringing over a decade of experience in engineering, project management, and international business. Originally from Colombia and now based in Australia, Brayan combines global perspective with a hands-on, client-focused approach.

After a successful corporate career, he launched RRB Group to offer tailored solutions in virtual offices, international trade, and property services. Known for his integrity, strategic mindset, and commitment to client success, Brayan leads the company with a clear mission: to help individuals and businesses grow through reliable, innovative services.
